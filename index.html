Pineapple-Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • One entire Philadelphia cream cheese
  • Two cans of pineapple
  • Small can of Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Graham cracker crusts

Steps:

  1. Put all ingredients together in a blender: Eggs, cream cheese, pineapple, condensed milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend into a puree.
  3. Pour the puree into the graham cracker crust.
  4. Note: The ingredients make two pineapple cheesecakes.
  5. Place the two pineapple cheesecakes in the fridge and allow solidification.
  6. Enjoy!
