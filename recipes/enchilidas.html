Enchilidas

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Queso Fresco
  • Chipotle Sauce
  1. Boil the chicken until fully cooked.
  2. While chicken is boiling, mix tomato and chipotle sauce.
  3. Crumble the queso fresco.
  4. Cut the lettuce.
  5. To facilitate the dish, you can buy a salsa of your spice tolerance.
  6. When the chicken is ready, shred the chicken.
  7. Get a pan and put some oil - enough to thinly coat the pan.
  8. Dip the tortilla in the tomato and chipotle sauce. Then place the tortilla in the pan to heat.
  9. Allow the tortillas to get warm and then pull the tortilla out and fill the tortilla with the chicken.
  10. Roll up the tortilla. Do the same for three to four tortillas. Then add the lettuce, queso fresco, and salsa.
  11. Enjoy!
