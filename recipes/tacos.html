Authentic Tacos

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas (no hardshells)
  • marinaded Carne asada (skirt steak, flap steak, flank steak
  • *Guacamole (store bought or homemade)
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Salsa
  • Grilled Green Onions
  • Radishes

Steps:

  1. Prep onions by fine dicing.
  2. Prep by thinly slicing radishes.
  3. Make pico de gallo with onion, cilantro, diced tomatoes, salt, and lemon juice. You may add serranos if you would like.
  4. Dice and cook the meat.
  5. Heat up the tortillas.
  6. Put the meat in the tortilla, add pico de gallo, guacamole, salsa, lemon.
  7. Have tacos with a side of radishes and grilled whole green onions.
